What are Heroes?

Heroes are playable characters with Overwatch that are also from lore. These characters can range from a cyborg ninja to a hamster on a wrecking ball. This wide variety of heroes can make the selection of what hero to play a difficult feat in their first match. The table below will briefly explain each character categorized by the class they are in.

The Heroes

Name Class Description
D. Va Tank D. Va is a mobile tank who works strongly in teams who move quickly. She is able to move around the terrain with ease. When the mech runs out of health, she pops out of the mech and can shoot her pistol to build up ult charge. Once she activates her ult out of her mech, she can resummon the mech at full health to continue fighting.
Orisa Tank Orisa is mechanical horse who is the anchor for a team. Using her shield and crowd control abilities, she can manipulate incoming damage as well as the enemies themselves. Unlike other tanks, she is best played at medium range where her rapid fire weapon is most succesful.
Reinhardt Tank Reinhardt is a large target but he is also a huge anchor to a team. Using his huge shield, he can block a ton of bullets, save his team from enemy ultimates, and help hold a point. He uses a big hammer to melee hit the enemy. The downside to using him though is that he has no resistance to crowd control abilities.
Roadhog Tank Roadhog is a tank that specializes in burst, close distance damage. He uses a scrap cannon that is similar to a shotgun. He has two abilities. The first is take a breather which allows Roadhog to heal himself and reduce damage taken during the time he uses the ability. The second ability is a hook that pulls a target in from of him to make them an easy target to eliminate.
Winston Tank Winston is a smart, talking gorilla from outer space. He is similar to D. Va in that he is mobile and should go after smaller targets using their mobility. Winston is able to use the jetpack on his back to propel himself into the air and shield in the shape of a bubble he can deploy. He is a close range tank as his weapon is a tesla that has a very small range.
Wrecking Ball Tank Wrecking ball is a big metal wercking ball with a hamster named Hammond in it. This tank is very mobile and is mainly a area denial/control tank. His guns are better at close range but can be used at medium to long range and Wrecking Ball uses a grappling hook to swing around. Once he has reached a certain speed, rolling into enemies will do damage.
Zarya Tank Zarya is a tank that excells at close range. She is able to place a bubble that protect herself and a bubble that can protect a teammate. They break after a certain amount of damage and only stay for a short amount of time. If an enemy hits the bubble, Zarya will gain charge which makes the beam coming from her weapon do more damage.
Bastion Damage Bastion is a robot that can utilize two different modes. The first mode is the assault mode where he is able to shoot with his gun and move around. The second mode is sentry mode where he becomes immobile but uses a machine gun that does and extremely high amount of damage. In both of these modes he can heal himself using another ability.
Doomfist Damage Doomfist is one of the Vilians in the Overwatch universe and his kit is made up of high mobility, hard hitting moves. He has a rocket punch, uppercut, and a slam. For each enemy he hits with one of these abilities, he gains shield so he can take a little bit more damage. If you punch someone into a wall as doomfist, it will do a lot more damage and usually one shot them.
Genji Damage Genji is one of the shimada brothers. He is a cyborg ninja who is very mobile. He shoots shurikens and can double jump. His other abilities include wall climbing, dashing very fast and doing damage with his sword, and deflecting bullets with his sword. With the dash, if he gets an elimination, the dash will automatically come off of cooldown.
Hanzo Damage Hanzo is the second of the shimada brother and is less mobile than genji. He is not a cyborg but instead of using shurikens, he uses a bow and arrow. Like genji, he can wall climb but instead of double jumping he can do a short dash that boosts him slightly. He is able to do a significant amount of damage if his arrows land.
Junkrat Damage Junkrat is a crazed maniac who uses a grenade launcher in battle. If the grenade hits someone, it will instantly explode. Otherwise, it will explode after a certain amount of time. He can use traps and mines to get from place to place and eliminate the ememy. He is mainly used to spam at the enemy to cause pressure to their supports.
Mccree Damage Mccree is a cowboy with one robotic arm. He uses a revolver with six shots in it to either do precise long range shots, or fan the hammer where he would fire a volley of close range shots in quick succession. He can also use a flashbang which stuns the enemy and can roll which allows him to instantly reload his weapon.
Mei Damage Mei is from antarctica and is known to be the "Ice Queen." She is able to freeze people with her primary fire and then shoot icicles that do a lot of damage with her secondary fire. She can also make an inpenitrable ice wall and can make a block of ice around her that stops her from moving but makes her invulnurable and also heals her.
Pharah Damage Pharah is a female in a rocket suit that allows her to fly into the air and deal damage form there. She has a roket launcher which fires rockets that do a lot of damage on a direct hit. She can also use her concussion blasts which knock back the enemy or herself giving her a little bit of crowd control and mobility.
Reaper Damage Reaper is another one of the vilians in the Overwatch lore. He uses shotguns to blast enemies at close range and can teleport slowly. He can also use a wraith form that makes him slightly faster and invinsible but also stops him from being able to shoot the enemy. When he reloads, he pulls a shotgun out of no where.
Soldier: 76 Damage Soldier: 76 is a soldier who uses a assault rifle that is best at medium range. He also carries a helix rocket with him that does a burst of damage and a healing station which heals a lot of damage in a large circle around wherever it is placed. He can also, unlike every other character, sprint or run around.
Sombra Damage Sombra is a stealth hacker who is made to flank and disrupt the enemy. She is able to make herself invisible to sneak up on the enemy. She can also hack health packs to make them better and only accessible to your team as well as hack the enemy which will disable their abilities for a short amount of time.
Symmetra Damage Symmetra is a hero who uses solid light to do damage. She uses a weapon that either makes balls that travel fast and do a lot of damage or shoots a beam that does more damage the longer it hits things. She can also make a teleporter and sentrys that can shoot beams that deal damage and slow the enemy.
Torbörn Damage Torbörn is a defensive hero who can throw a turret that auto builds and does automatically aimed damage to the enemy. He can also use and ability called overload where all his actions speed up, he does more damage, and he gains a bunch of armor. He is better used at control points where he is able to set up at long distance but engage at short distance.
Tracer Damage Tracer is a very mobile hero who does her job effectively. She is able to do short teleports that happen instantly and can use an ability called recal where she goes back 5 seconds to wherever she was then and whatever health she was at at the point. She has the smallest default health pool but is best at flanking and eliminating the enemy supports.
Widowmaker Damage Widowmaker is a long range sniper who uses a dual purpose weapon. Her weapon can either be a sniper that does more damage the longer she is scoped in or as an assault rifle that does a small amount of damage but has a wide spread. To get around to she has a grappling hook and also can throw venom mines that do a small amount of damage to enemies.
Ana Damage Symmetra is a hero who uses solid light to do damage. She uses a weapon that either makes balls that travel fast and do a lot of damage or shoots a beam that does more damage the longer it hits things. She can also make a teleporter and sentrys that can shoot beams that deal damage and slow the enemy.
