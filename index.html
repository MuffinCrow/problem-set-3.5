Who created Overwatch?

Overwatch was created by a small team in a company called Blizzard. The team is led by director and vice president of Blizzard, Jeff Kaplan who makes updates on the Overwatch YouTube channel. The other people on the team are:

Jeff Kaplan Game Director and Vice President
Chris Metzen Senior Vice President
Arnold Tsang and Ben Zhang Conceptual Artists
Scott Mercer and Geoff Goodman Principal Designers
Jeremy Craig Senior Game Designer
Michael Elliott Senior Technical Director
Bill Petras Art Director
Aaron Keller Assistant Game Director
Alyssa Wong Lead Story Writer
Andrew Wang Senior Software Engineer
Kim-Seang "Nesskain" Hong Artist who will be joining the team this year
